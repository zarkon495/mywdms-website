Access Management

Control Who Sees What — Down to the Smallest Detail

Most legacy waste management software gives everyone access to everything, or nothing at all. myWDMS lets you define exactly what each person on your team can see, edit, and act on.

  • Role-based permissions for Admins, Dispatchers, Drivers, Billing, and custom roles
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) via email or text for all users
  • Permissions-based database switching for users managing multiple locations or divisions
  • Invite users by email with automatic role assignment
  • Audit trail of every login, permission change, and user action

Route Management

Manage Every Stop, Every Driver, Every Day

Stop relying on paper manifests and phone calls. myWDMS gives dispatchers a live view of every route, and drivers the clarity they need to get the job done efficiently.

  • Create and assign service routes to drivers in seconds
  • Track route progress in real time from any device
  • Manage residential, commercial, and roll-off stops in one place
  • Create and manage special service and dispatch tickets for on-demand requests
  • Flag missed pickups, delays, and exceptions automatically
  • Route history and completion logs stored for compliance

Lockbox & Billing

Stop Chasing Payments. Start Closing Your Books Faster.

Legacy billing systems are slow, error-prone, and disconnected from the rest of your operation. myWDMS brings payment collection, invoicing, and reconciliation into one place.

  • Integrated lockbox for processing mailed payments automatically
  • Generate and send invoices directly from the platform
  • Manage billing requests and process rate changes across your entire account base
  • Account groups and divisions for multi-department or multi-contract billing
  • Customizable statement messages per account or billing cycle
  • Real-time cash collection dashboard for owners and billing staff
  • Payment history tied directly to customer accounts
  • Export-ready for your accounting software

Logs & Reporting

Real-Time Visibility Across Your Entire Business

Know exactly what's happening at every level of your operation — right now. myWDMS gives you live dashboards, detailed audit logs, and exportable reports your accountants and compliance teams will love.

  • Live operational dashboard with key metrics at a glance
  • Full audit log of every user action with timestamp and IP
  • Route completion reports, billing summaries, and collection history
  • Customizable report templates saved per user or role
  • Export to CSV, PDF, or connect to your BI tool
