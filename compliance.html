myWDMS handles payment and billing data for waste haulers and municipalities across the United States. We take that responsibility seriously — which is why we maintain PCI DSS Level 1 Compliance, the most stringent certification in the payments industry.

What is PCI DSS?

The PCI Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) applies to all entities that store, process, and/or transmit cardholder data. It covers technical and operational practices for system components included in or connected to environments with cardholder data. If you accept or process payment cards, PCI DSS applies to you.

What is Level 1 Compliance?

Level 1 is the highest tier of PCI DSS certification. It applies to service providers that store, process, or transmit more than 300,000 credit card transactions annually. Meeting Level 1 requirements includes:

  • All PCI DSS requirements validated in full
  • Annual Report on Compliance (ROC) by a Qualified Security Assessor (QSA)
  • Quarterly network scans by an Approved Scanning Vendor (ASV)
  • Annual penetration testing of network and application layers
  • Internal security scans across all cardholder data environments

Data Encryption

All cardholder data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard TLS and AES-256 encryption.

Annual Audits

We undergo a full audit by a Qualified Security Assessor every year — not just when required, but as a standard part of how we operate.

Continuous Monitoring

Our infrastructure is monitored 24/7 with quarterly ASV scans and real-time alerting for any anomalous access patterns.
