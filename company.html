How myWDMS Came to Be

It started with a conversation. Our parent company, myonlinebill, had been processing payments for waste and utility companies for years. And every single time we'd sit down with a hauler or a municipal billing department, we'd hear the same thing: "Our operations software is a nightmare."

They weren't exaggerating. We saw systems from the late 1990s still running dispatch for fleets of 60 trucks. We saw billing managers manually reconciling payments in Excel at 9 PM on a Friday. We saw drivers navigating with printed paper manifests.

We knew the payment side of the business. We understood the compliance requirements, the billing complexity, the operational rhythm of waste collection. So we built myWDMS — not as a side project, but as a dedicated platform designed to be the system the industry has needed for two decades.

Today, myWDMS powers operations across the country, from single-location haulers to multi-depot municipal contracts. And we're just getting started.
